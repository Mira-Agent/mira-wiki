# Agentic Wallet

<Badge variant="secondary">Coming Soon</Badge>

Mira's Agentic Wallet brings blockchain transactions directly into your Telegram conversations. Connect your wallet, check balances, send payments, and swap tokens — no separate app needed.

## What is Agentic Wallet?

Agentic Wallet is Mira's built-in TON blockchain wallet. It lives inside your Telegram chat and lets you perform on-chain actions through natural language.

No apps to download. No addresses to copy. Just tell Mira what you want.

## Connect your wallet

You can connect an existing TON wallet:

```
Connect my TON wallet
```

Or Mira can create a new wallet for you. Either way, you stay in control.

## What you can do

### Check balance

```
What's my TON balance?
How much USDT do I have?
Show my wallet
```

### Send transactions

```
Send 5 TON to EQC...
Transfer 10 USDT to @username
```

### Swap tokens

```
Swap 2 TON to USDT
What's the current TON/USDT rate?
```

### Transaction history

```
Show my recent transactions
What did I send last week?
```

## TON ecosystem

The Agentic Wallet is built on **TON (The Open Network)** — a fast, scalable blockchain integrated with Telegram.

- **Lightning-fast** — transactions confirm in seconds
- **Low fees** — fractions of a cent per transaction
- **Telegram-native** — TON is built into the Telegram ecosystem
- **Growing ecosystem** — DeFi, NFTs, staking, and more

## Agentic future

The Agentic Wallet is the first step toward **agent-to-agent transactions**:

- **Pay other agents** for services — imagine Mira paying another AI agent for specialized research
- **Agent commerce** — AI agents negotiating and transacting autonomously on your behalf
- **Cross-agent collaboration** — agents exchanging value for data, compute, and expertise
- **Smart triggers** — "When my wallet receives TON, send me a notification" or "Auto-swap USDT when balance drops below 10"

This is the foundation of an **agentic economy** — where AI agents are not just tools, but economic participants.

## Time-saving workflows

### Sprint management

```
Create a Linear task: "Fix payment flow on mobile" with priority High
What are my overdue tasks?
Move MIRA-123 to In Review
Summarize sprint progress this week
```

### Research & analysis

```
What are the latest trends in AI agents?
Compare our competitor's pricing page with ours
Summarize this 10-page PDF into 5 bullet points
```

### Writing & communication

```
Draft a LinkedIn post about our new feature launch
Write an investor update for March
Rewrite this email to be more concise
```

### Decision support

```
Help me decide: should we build or buy this tool?
List pros and cons of launching in the US first
What are the risks of this approach?
```

## Popular use cases

- **PRD drafting** — describe a feature, get a structured PRD
- **Competitive analysis** — search and summarize competitor moves
- **Bug triage** — categorize bugs by severity and assign owners
- **Metrics review** — paste numbers, get insights and trends
- **Pitch deck feedback** — send slides, get improvement suggestions
- **1-on-1 prep** — generate talking points for team meetings
- **Release notes** — summarize merged PRs into user-facing notes

<Tip>Create a skill called "PM Assistant" that combines your most common workflows into one command.</Tip>

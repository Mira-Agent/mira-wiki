# Video

Mira can generate short video clips from text, animate images, and download videos from the internet.

## Generate videos

Describe a scene and Mira will generate a short video clip. Costs **100 tokens**.

```
Generate a video of a sunset over the ocean with gentle waves
```

### Aspect ratios
- `9:16` — vertical (default, great for Reels/Stories)
- `16:9` — horizontal (great for YouTube/presentations)

## Image to video

Send an image and ask Mira to animate it:

```
Animate this photo — make the hair flow in the wind
```

Costs **100 tokens**.

## Download videos

Send Mira an Instagram Reel or direct video URL:

```
https://www.instagram.com/reel/ABC123/
```

Mira will download and send the video directly in chat.

## Video notes (circles)

Send a video and ask Mira to convert it to a Telegram video note (the round circle format):

```
Convert this to a video note
```

## Create Shorts

Send Mira a YouTube or social media video URL and ask her to create short clips:

```
Create shorts from this video: https://youtube.com/watch?v=...
```

Mira will analyze the video and generate multiple viral-ready clips with subtitles and headlines. Processing takes 5–15 minutes.

## Tokens

<Info>
  Video generation costs **100 🪙 tokens**. Downloading and converting videos is free.
</Info>

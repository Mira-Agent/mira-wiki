## Voice Messages

This is where Mira truly shines. **Voice is the most loved feature by our users** — and for good reason.

### Why users love Mira's voice

- **Zero friction** — press and hold to record, just like talking to a friend. No keyboard needed.
- **Instant transcription** — Mira converts your speech to text in real time, understands context, and replies.
- **Voice replies** — ask Mira to reply with a voice message and she'll generate natural-sounding audio.
- **Hands-free workflow** — dictate tasks, ask questions, get reminders while walking, cooking, commuting.
- **Multilingual** — speak in any language, Mira understands and responds in kind.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Record a voice message">
    Use Telegram's built-in voice recorder — just hold the mic button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mira transcribes and understands">
    Mira automatically converts your speech to text and processes it with full context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a reply">
    Mira responds in text by default. Ask *"reply with voice"* to hear her answer aloud.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Text to Speech

Ask Mira to read any text aloud:

```
Read this to me as a voice message: [your text]
```

## Conversation Memory

Mira remembers important things about you across conversations:

- Your **name, location, occupation**
- Your **preferences and interests**
- **Facts** you share (e.g. "I prefer short answers", "I'm learning Spanish")

<Note>
  Memory is private by default. In group chats, facts are visible to other members.
</Note>

## Web Search

Mira can search the internet for current information — news, prices, weather, research:

```
What's the weather in London today?
Latest news about AI regulation in the EU
Find the best ramen restaurants in Tokyo
```
